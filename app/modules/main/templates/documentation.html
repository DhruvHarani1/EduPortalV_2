Welcome to EduPortal

The complete operating system for modern educational institutions.

EduPortal is a unified platform designed to bridge the gap between administration, faculty, and students. By centralizing data and workflows, it ensures that everyone stays connected and informed.

Whether you are an administrator managing hundreds of courses, a faculty member tracking attendance, or a student checking your exam results, EduPortal provides a tailored experience for your role.

System Architecture

EduPortal follows a modular Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture, powered by Flask (Python) on the backend and modern HTML5/TailwindCSS on the frontend.

Core Components

  • Authentication Layer: Secure login with role-based access control (RBAC) ensuring users only see what they need.
  • Database: Relational database schema managing complex relationships between Students, Courses, Attendance, and Exams.
  • Reporting Engine: Generates real-time PDF reports and visual analytics for data-driven decision making.

Admin Dashboard Overview

The Admin Dashboard is the command center of EduPortal. Upon logging in, administrators are greeted with high-level metrics:

  • Total Students & Faculty: Real-time headcount.
  • Course Distribution: Visual breakdown of student enrollment by course.
  • Recent Activity: Audit log of latest system notices and updates.

Pro Tip: Use the "System Report Print" feature in the top-right to generate a PDF snapshot of these metrics for board meetings.

User Management

Admins have full control over the institution's roster.

Managing Students

You can add, edit, or remove student profiles. Key data points include:

  • Personal details (Name, DOB, Contact)
  • Academic Info (Course, Year, Enrollment Date)
  • Parent/Guardian Contact Info

Managing Faculty

Faculty profiles include designation, department, and assigned courses. Deleting a faculty member will intelligently prompt to reassign their classes.

System Reports

Generate comprehensive reports to track institutional health.

Report Type Description
Student Performance Aggregate view of grades across all courses.
Attendance Logs Daily, weekly, and monthly attendance trends.
Fee Status List of paid, pending, and overdue fees.

Marking Attendance

The "My Classes" section allows faculty to view their schedule and mark daily attendance.

  1. Navigate to Attendance in the sidebar.
  2. Select the Course and Date.
  3. You will see a checklist of students. Mark those who are Present (default is Absent).
  4. Click Save Attendance.

Attendance data is immediately available to students in their dashboard.

Grading & Syllabus

Uploading Syllabus

Keep students informed by uploading the latest course syllabus (PDF format supported). This appears in the Student's "Academics" tab.

Entering Marks

Go to the Marks section to input exam results. You can enter marks for internal assessments, mid-terms, and final exams. The system automatically calculates the GPA based on credit weightage.

Mentorship Program

EduPortal fosters close guidance through its Mentorship module.

  • Assigned Mentees: View the list of students assigned to you for guidance.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor their attendance and academic performance.
  • Query Resolution: Respond to private queries sent by your mentees regarding academic or personal issues.

Using the Student Portal

Your personal dashboard gives you a snapshot of your campus life.

  • Timetable: View your daily class schedule.
  • Notices: Important announcements from the admin office.
  • Quick Links: Fast access to Fees, Library, and Exams.

Academics & Exams

Attendance Tracking

Check your attendance percentage per subject. If your attendance drops below 75%, it will be highlighted in red.

Examination Results

View your marksheet for completed semesters. You can download a PDF copy of your marksheet for official use.

Student Services

Access essential campus services directly from the portal:

  • Fee Payment: View pending dues and pay online securely. Download receipts instantly.
  • Scholarships: Apply for merit-based or need-based scholarships. Track your application status.
  • ID Card: Report a lost ID card and request a replacement.
  • Event Registration: Browse upcoming university events and register with a single click.

Advanced Workflows

Exam Management Lifecycle

The examination process in EduPortal follows a strict 5-stage lifecycle to ensure integrity and smooth conduct.

1

Event Creation (Admin)

Admin creates an "Exam Event" (e.g., "Mid-Term Spring 2024"). This defines the date range and eligible batches.

2

Scheduling (Admin)

Dates are assigned to specific subjects. The system automatically checks for slot conflicts.

3

Hall Ticket Generation (System)

Once the schedule is locked, students can download their Hall Tickets from the portal.

4

Grading (Faculty)

After exams, faculty enter marks. The system validates entries against maximum marks defined in the syllabus.

5

Result Publication (Admin)

Admin reviews the "Result Compilation Report" and clicks "Publish". Results are then visible to students.

Fee Structure & Payment

EduPortal simplifies financial operations with a transparent fee management system.

For Students/Parents

  • Notifications: Email alerts 7 days before due date.
  • Payment Gateways: Support for Credit Card, Net Banking, and UPI.
  • Instant Receipts: PDF receipts generated immediately after successful transaction.

For Admins

  • Fee Heads: configure components (Tuition, Library, Lab).
  • Defaulter List: One-click report of students with outstanding dues.
  • Reconciliation: Daily settlement reports matched with bank statements.

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Error 403: Access Denied

Cause: You represent trying to access a page not authorized for your user role (e.g., a Student trying to access Faculty grading).

Fix: Log out and log in with the correct credentials. If you believe this is a mistake, contact the Admin.

Login Failed

Cause: Incorrect password or account blocked due to multiple failed attempts.

Fix: Use the "Forgot Password" link or ask the Admin to reset your credentials from the User Management dashboard.
